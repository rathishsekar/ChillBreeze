About Us

Chillbreeze Salons and Spas Chennai is one of the top Beauty Salons and Spas, offering a diverse range of services for men and women. As one of the best Beauty salons and most award-winning spas in Chennai we are eager to make your experience with us a great.

We're progressive and chic, with a first-rate reputation and a friendly, nurturing staff. We dedicate ourselves to delivering the best , most up-to-date haircuts, special occasion styling, hair colour and treatments available.

It is Chillbreeze mission to give guests a great experience, while striving for excellence in customer care and retail. Chillbreeze will keep a strong base of team-oriented employees, creating an open, non-competitive environment, so that our guests can enjoy the convenience of working with any one of our talented team members. We will maintain the leading edge in current and upcoming styles with advanced education. By paying special attention to the guests' needs, Chillbreeze is recapturing the lost art of "customer service".
